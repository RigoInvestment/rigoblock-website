RigoBlock Vision

We believe Blockchain technology will be at the backbone of modern financial markets' infrastructure.

RigoBlock Mission

Our purpose is to organize pools of digital tokens, democratizing access to the hedge fund club for everyone.

RigoBlock Decentralized Applications

Drago

Create a Drago, your own decentralized hedge fund! Dragos can trade on decentralized exchanges and allow for instant management of subscriptions and redemptions.

Exchange

RigoBlock comes with a built-in decentralized exchange for leveraged derivatives trading. External liquidity providers can create their own darkpool on top of our exchange.

Complete Decentralization

The RigoBlock protocol is already live on testnets and entirely built on Ethereum: our platform runs also fully decentralized on IPFS. Read our blog for more.

High Speed and Low Cost

RigoBlock is the first and most comprehensive structure of decentralized pools of digital tokens. It allows instant creation of pools for free. Currently in alpha on the Kovan testnet, worldwide accessible in the Parity UI.

Vault

Create a Vault, your own trustless custodian: organize your Family&Friends' Ether holdings. Vaults allow for Ethereum pooled Proof-of-Stake mining with Casper.

Registry

We've created the first global registry for decentralized hedge funds. External asset managers can use the RigoBlock Registry and create their own decentralized hedge fund platform on top of RigoBlock.

Fund Standards

RigoBlock is built on top of our Smart Contracts Protocol created following Conditioned Oriented Programming.

Proof of Performance

Proof-of-Performance is the RigoBlock minting algorithm, rewarding traders for the quality of their performance. Users willing to participate to the RigoBlock network incentives must hold Rigo tokens.
